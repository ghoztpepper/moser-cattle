Our Story

Four Generations. 125 Years. One Piece of Texas Land.

Like so many who came before us, our family arrived in America with little more than hope and an unwavering determination to build something lasting. In 1900, they put down roots in the raw, unforgiving landscape of Clay County in North Texas, just south of the Red River, and began carving a life from the land.

Through farming and raising livestock, they built a homestead, fed their children, and weathered everything this country could throw at them: drought, wildfire, brutal winters, and the weight of the Great Depression. They endured not through luck, but through grit, ingenuity, and an unshakeable belief that if you took care of the land, the land would take care of you.

That philosophy wasn't a marketing slogan. It was survival. And generation by generation, it became legacy.

Today, as a fourth-generation cattle ranching family, we carry that legacy forward. We honor the traditional practices our forefathers refined over decades while embracing the modern tools and technology that make us better stewards of the land they loved. The challenges haven't disappeared; ranching never promised easy. But the commitment hasn't either.

For 125 years, hard work and deep respect for this land have defined our family. They still do.

The Herd

Genetics & Breeding

The Bulls

Our commercial cow herd is built on proven Angus bloodlines like DHD Traveler 6807 and N Bar Emulation EXT. We use artificial insemination with top sires such as Tehama Tahoe B767 and Stellpflug Hoffman RiseAbove to enhance genetics. Our goal is simple: cattle that will thrive in our environment and perform well on limited inputs.

We select bulls for structural soundness, strong feet, growth, and superior carcass traits.

Our grain-finished steers are sold directly to consumers as locally raised, hormone-free, antibiotic-free beef under our Natural Choice Beef™ brand — humanely raised and full of flavor.

Herd Development

The Heifers

We offer a select number of bred heifers and 1st calf heifer pairs each year. All heifers are bred to calving ease bulls with good growth and carcass merits — giving you a valuable calf from the beginning.

Strict measurement and tracking of growth help us eliminate those that don't perform up to our standards. We follow a complete health and vaccination program which includes a calfhood Bangs vaccine so the heifers can be transported anywhere in the US.
